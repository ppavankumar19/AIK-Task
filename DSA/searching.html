Shad-rasa andDosha Connection

(Six Tastes and How They Affect theDoshas)

Ayurveda teaches that every food has one or more of the six tastes (Shad-rasa), and each taste can either increase or decrease the threeDoshasVta, Pitta, and Kapha. This is why what we eat has a big effect on how we feel.

========================================================================


1. Sweet (Madhura)

Increases Kapha

Decreases Vta and Pitta

Example: Rice, milk, banana make the body calm and strong but too much makes it heavy.


========================================================================

2. Sour (Amla)

Increases Pitta and Kapha

Decreases Vta

Example: Lemon and curd improve digestion but too much causes heat.


========================================================================

3. Salty (Lavaa)

Increases Pitta and Kapha

Decreases Vta

Example: Salt in food helps absorption but too much makes the body warm and swollen.


========================================================================

4. Bitter (Tikta)

Decreases Pitta and Kapha

Slightly increases Vta

Example: Bitter gourd cleans the body but can feel drying.


========================================================================

5. Pungent/Spicy (Kau)

Increases Pitta and Vta

Decreases Kapha

Example: Pepper and ginger heat the body and wake up digestion.


========================================================================

6. Astringent (Kaya)

Decreases Pitta and Kapha

Increases Vta

Example: Raw banana, pomegranate, and beans dry up excess moisture.

========================================================================


Overall Understanding

  1. Vta likes sweet, sour, and salty foods; gets disturbed by spicy, bitter, and astringent foods.


  1. Pitta likes sweet, bitter, and astringent foods; gets disturbed by spicy, sour, and salty foods.


  1. Kapha likes spicy, bitter, and astringent foods; gets disturbed by sweet, sour, and salty foods.

========================================================================



Simple Rule for Students

The taste of the food can balance or disturb aDosha. Eating the right tastes keeps the mind calm and the body healthy.
