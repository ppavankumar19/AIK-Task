Understanding Prakti Your Natural Body Type

  1. Prakti means your natural mindbody constitution.
  2. It is formed by a unique mix of Vta, Pitta, and Kapha at birth.
  3. It decides how your body works, how your mind thinks, and what keeps you healthy.
  4. No two people have the same Praktijust like fingerprints.

Vta Prakti The Creative & Energetic Type

Common Qualities:

  1. Light and active body
  2. Fast movements and quick thinking
  3. Creative, imaginative, loves new ideas
  4. Talks a lot and enjoys exploring


When Out of Balance:

  1. Feels anxious or worried
  2. Forgets things easily
  3. Feels tired, dry, or cold


What Helps:

  1. Warm food and drinks
  2. Sleeping on time
  3. A steady daily routine
  4. Calm, grounding activities



Pitta Prakti The Smart & Focused Type

Common Qualities:

  1. Sharp intelligence, good concentration
  2. Strong hunger and warm body
  3. Natural leadership qualities
  4. Enjoys challenges and competition


When Out of Balance:

  1. Feels angry or irritated
  2. Gets overheated or sweaty
  3. Becomes too demanding or perfectionistic

What Helps:

  1. Cooling foods and drinks
  2. Staying hydrated
  3. Spending time in nature
  4. Gentle, relaxing activities



Kapha Prakti The Calm & Steady Type

Common Qualities:

  1. Strong, steady body
  2. Calm, patient, gentle nature
  3. Good stamina and memory
  4. Likes routine and comfort


When Out of Balance:

  1. Feels heavy, sleepy, or lazy
  2. Has excess mucus or slow digestion
  3. Finds it hard to get motivated

What Helps:

  1. Light exercise and movement
  2. Warm, spicy foods
  3. Fun, energising activities
  4. Staying active and engaged



Mixed Praktis (Two Doshas Together)

  1. Most people are VtaPitta, PittaKapha, or VtaKapha.
  2. They show qualities of both Doshas.
  3. Example: A VtaPitta child may be creative AND sharp.
  4. Understanding mixed Praktis helps choose better foods and routines.



Why Knowing Your Prakti Helps

  1. You learn what foods give you energy
  2. You understand which activities balance you
  3. You become aware of early signs of imbalance
  4. You can take care of your body and mind better
